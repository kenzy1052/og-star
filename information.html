ABOUT US

Founded in 2019, O G STAR Travel and Tours is a certified, accredited, and trusted travel company dedicated to creating exceptional experiences for our clients. We specialize in guided tours, sightseeing trips, and personalized travel experiences within Ghana and beyond.

Our work and stay abroad programs connect clients with trustworthy organizations in Abu Dhabi and Dubai that are hiring in various fields. We guide our clients through every step — from job matching and interview preparation to visa assistance, travel planning, and final relocation.

At O G STAR, we take pride in security, integrity, and customer satisfaction. We ensure every client connects only with legitimate companies and verified opportunities, making their journey safe, transparent, and professional.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is O G STAR a licensed and accredited travel company?

Yes, O G STAR Travel and Tours is fully licensed, certified, and accredited. We operate in compliance with all travel industry regulations and standards, ensuring our clients receive professional and trustworthy services.

Do you offer tours within Ghana?

Absolutely! We specialize in organizing guided tours and sightseeing trips throughout Ghana. From historical sites like Cape Coast Castle to natural wonders like Mole National Park, we offer comprehensive tours that showcase Ghana's rich culture and beauty.

Can O G STAR help me find work opportunities abroad?

Yes, our work and stay abroad programs connect clients with trustworthy organizations in Abu Dhabi and Dubai that are hiring in various fields. We provide end-to-end support, from job matching and interview preparation to visa assistance and relocation.

Which countries do you currently handle for work and stay abroad?

Currently, we focus on work opportunities in Abu Dhabi and Dubai. We have established partnerships with verified companies in these locations and are continuously expanding our network to include more destinations.

How secure is the process of working abroad with O G STAR?

Security is our top priority. We thoroughly vet all partner companies and opportunities to ensure they are legitimate and reliable. Our team provides transparent guidance throughout the entire process, and we maintain open communication to address any concerns.

Do you assist with visa processing and interview preparation?

Yes, we provide comprehensive visa assistance and interview preparation services. Our experienced team guides clients through the entire visa application process and offers personalized coaching to help them succeed in interviews.

Can I customize my travel package?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized travel experiences tailored to your preferences, budget, and interests. Our travel consultants work closely with you to design the perfect itinerary for your journey.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, mobile money, and major credit/debit cards. Payment plans are also available for certain services to make our offerings more accessible. Our team will provide detailed payment information during the booking process.
